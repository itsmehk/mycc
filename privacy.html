Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CardMatch Pro collects minimal information necessary to provide personalized credit card recommendations:

  • Name (first name only)
  • Annual income range
  • Credit score range
  • Monthly spending amount
  • Spending categories and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Calculate personalized credit card recommendations
  • Display expected benefits based on your spending patterns
  • Filter cards based on your eligibility

3. Data Storage and Security

Your quiz responses are processed locally in your browser and are not stored on our servers. We do not maintain a database of user information.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. We have no partnerships or affiliate agreements with banks or financial institutions.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for essential functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or share data with advertising networks.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't store your data, there is nothing to delete or modify. Each quiz session is independent and your data is cleared when you close your browser.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@cardmatch.pro
