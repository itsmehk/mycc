Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MyCC

Is MyCC free to use?
Yes! MyCC is 100% free for users. We earn through affiliate partnerships with banks when you apply through our recommendations, but this never affects our matching algorithm or your experience.
Does checking my match affect my credit score?
Not at all! Using MyCC to find card matches does not impact your credit score. We don't perform hard credit checks. Your score is only affected when you officially apply for a card.
How accurate are the card matches?
Our matching algorithm has a 95% approval accuracy rate. We consider your income, credit profile, spending patterns, and current card portfolio to provide highly personalized recommendations based on actual bank criteria.
How do you earn money if the service is free?
We earn a small commission from banks when you successfully apply for a card through our platform. This affiliate model keeps our service free for you while ensuring our recommendations remain unbiased and based solely on what's best for your needs.
